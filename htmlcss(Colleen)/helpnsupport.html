Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Account Setup & Verification

Q: How do I create an account on Campus Connect?

A: You can create an account by signing up with your WMSU-validated school ID and filling in your basic details. Verification is mandatory for access.

Q: Why is verification required?

A: Verification ensures that only WMSU students and employees use the platform, promoting a safe and secure environment.

2. Buying & Selling

Q: How do I list an item for sale?

A: Go to your dashboard and click on “List an Item.” Add a product description, price, and images before publishing.

Q: How do I purchase an item?

A: Use the search and filter options to find items. Click “Buy Now” or “Add to Cart” and proceed with payment.

Q: Can I trade items instead of buying/selling?

A: Yes, Campus Connect offers a trading feature. You can initiate a trade by selecting the item and offering an exchange.

3. Payment & Transactions

Q: What payment methods are accepted?

A: Payments are processed through secure third-party services. You can use debit/credit cards or e-wallets.

Q: How do I withdraw earnings?

A: Sellers can cash out their earnings from the Wallet section in the dashboard. Ensure your bank details are updated.

Q: Are there any fees for transactions?

A: Campus Connect charges a commission based on the transaction amount:

  • ₱1 to ₱500 – 2%
  • ₱501 to ₱1,000 – 5%
  • ₱1,001 to ₱10,000 – 8%
  • ₱10,001 to ₱20,000 – 12%
  • ₱20,001 and above – 15%

4. Managing Orders & Cancellations

Q: How can I cancel an order?

A: Go to My Orders and select the order you wish to cancel. Choose a reason from the list, such as:

  • I changed my mind.
  • Wrong address.
  • Found a better deal.

Q: What happens if a seller doesn't confirm the sale?

A: Sellers have a set time to confirm orders. If they fail to do so, they may receive a warning, and a penalty of 0.5% of the transaction amount may be deducted.

5. Reporting Issues

Q: How do I report a problem with a transaction?

A: Navigate to Help & Support in the dashboard and click on “Report an Issue.” Provide the transaction details and a description of the issue.

Q: What should I do if I encounter a scam or fraudulent listing?

A: Report the listing immediately using the “Report” button on the product page. Our team will review and take action.
